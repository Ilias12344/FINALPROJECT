Understanding Chest Pain

Chest pain can be caused by many factors, including heart problems, stress, or lifestyle choices. Keeping track of how often the pain occurs, how severe it is, and what might trigger it can help you and bear in mind it can make changes with visiting your doctor. This app allows you to record your chest pain, making it easier to track and manage over time.
