The purpose

This application was developed to support individuals experiencing various types of pain they are affected from and the benefical side of it as a tool. The purpose is to help individuals better understand and track their pain symptoms continously such as chest pain, back pain, joint pain, headaches, and abdominal pain. The application allows individual to record, monitor, and reflect on their pain journeys in a meaningful way.

The Mission

The mission of this application is to give individuals insights over their health by offering a clear and simple platform to log, track, and analyse pain symptoms. Whether someone is managing chronic pain, the goal is to help them gain useful, identify patterns, and improve the progress with their health.
